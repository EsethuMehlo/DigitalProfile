Reflection — STAR Technique

S — Situation:

No formal mock interview was provided in my module, so I initiated a self-directed simulation to practice responses and evaluate readiness.

T — Task:

My task was to identify weaknesses in my interview communication and develop strategies to improve.

A — Action:

I created sample interview questions, practiced answering them aloud, recorded myself, and reviewed online mock interview examples to benchmark professional delivery.

R — Result:

The simulation revealed specific improvements required (clearer technical explanations, improved pacing). It increased my confidence and prepared me better for actual interviews.
